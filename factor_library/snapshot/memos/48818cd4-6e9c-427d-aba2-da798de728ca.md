# Factor Research Memo: hedging_language_intensity

## Summary

This factor measures the density of hedging language in a company's 10-K filing, which may indicate a higher level of uncertainty or risk. The basic idea is that increased use of hedging language can signal to investors that a company's prospects are less certain, leading to lower future returns. As a result, the factor predicts lower forward returns for companies with higher hedging language intensity.

## Hypothesis & Rationale

- **Description:** Measures the density of hedging language in a company's 10-K filing

- **Economic rationale:** Management's increased use of hedging language may indicate a higher level of uncertainty or risk, which can negatively impact future returns; as a result, higher hedging language intensity predicts lower forward returns as investors become more cautious about the company's prospects.
