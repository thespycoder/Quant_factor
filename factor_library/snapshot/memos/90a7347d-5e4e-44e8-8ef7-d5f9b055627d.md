# Factor Research Memo: growth_sentiment

## Summary

The growth_sentiment factor measures the relative frequency of growth and positive language in a company's filings, with the hypothesis that a higher ratio of growth to positive language signals increased investor confidence and positive returns.

## Hypothesis & Rationale

- **Description:** Measures the relative frequency of growth and positive language
- **Computation:** ratio(`phrase:growth`, `lm_category:LM_positive`), normalized by filing length
- **Direction:** +1    **Horizon:** fwd_ret_63d    **Universe:** all
