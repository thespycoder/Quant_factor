# Factor Research Memo: regulatory_headwind_index

## Summary

The regulatory headwind index measures the frequency of regulation-related language in a company's 10-K filing, capturing potential regulatory challenges that may impact future returns. This factor aims to identify companies facing increasing regulatory scrutiny, which can lead to stricter compliance requirements, fines, or operational restrictions. The index is designed to indicate a higher likelihood of regulatory headwinds.

## Hypothesis & Rationale

- **Description:** Captures the relative frequency of regulation language in a company's 10-K filing
- **Computation:** sum(`phrase:regulation`), normalized by filing length
- **Direction:** -1    **Horizon:** fwd_ret_21d    **Universe:** all
- **Economic rationale:** Companies facing increasing regulatory scrutiny may be subject to stricter compliance requirements, fines, or even operational restrictions. The relative frequency of regulation language can indicate a higher likelihood of regulatory headwinds, ultimately resulting in negative forward returns.
